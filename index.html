My Trip to Europe

The Journey Begins!

July 22nd, 2017

My girlfriend Maddy and I started our trip in Chicago where my her family lives. We were traveling to Scotland for a wedding and wanted to make the most of the trip so we added a few pit stops along the way.

First Stop London

July 22nd-24th, 2017

The first two days of our trip were spent in London exploring with Maddy's Cousin, the bride to be and her future groom. We saw the Thames, Colombia Flower Market, Abbey Road Studios, and the British Museum

Paris

July 24th-27th, 2017

We took a train to Paris, France where we stayed for three days. We visited many historic sites, saw live music, ate great food, and walked until we couldnt.

Scotland

July 27th-August 2nd, 2017

As hard as it was to leave Paris behind, we were on to bigger and better things. The wedding was held on the Skibo Castle grounds west of Dornoch, Scotland. After the wedding festivities were over we traveled to Dornie where we stayed and hiked for three days.
